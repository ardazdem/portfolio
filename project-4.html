Overview

In this concept, I aimed to enhance the tactile feedback of modern oven interfaces, addressing the challenge of transferring the intuitive physical interaction of traditional ovens to digital interfaces. I conducted usability testing with a cardboard prototype featuring a central control wheel, allowing users to select settings and navigate through basic functions.

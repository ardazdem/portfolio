Atlas

Overview

Atlas is a sleep-regulating smart self-care device designed to adapt comfort-inducing toys to help adults improve sleep quality. It monitors key biometric data, such as heart rate and breathing frequency, to guide the user's sleep. Atlas targets common issues like difficulty falling asleep and disrupted sleep patterns, which can lead to long-term sleep problems.

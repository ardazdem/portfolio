I.WAS.MADE.IN.A.LAB

Overview

This is an exploration of sustainable fashion, focusing on the repurposing of textiles to bridge the gap between past and present fashion trends. This project delves into the intersection of environmental consciousness and sartorial creativity, offering a tangible manifestation of how older fashion trends can be rejuvenated to craft contemporary aesthetics.
